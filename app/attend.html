TEDxUofW 2016

Thank you for attending TEDxUofW 2016!

Our theme this year was "In Motion", where we hoped to showcase the ever-changing nature of human activity in the Seattle community. To be in motion means to constantly evolve, and what we, TEDxUofW, strive to show is our devotion to the progress that is so evident within our local and global sphere in a University setting.

To learn more about the event and to watch video recordings of the talks, please see our archive.
